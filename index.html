Who We Are

Founded in 2016 in Kalamazoo, MI

ROC is a volunteer-run non profit where compassion meets action.

For years, our educational workshops, U.S. collegiate campus initiatives, and tutoring resettlement services is what sets us apart. Find out why local and global communities trust our expertise in advocacy, community support, and making a difference through education.

LEARN MORE

What We Do

We have joined the global movement to make education accessible and equitable to people as they migrate across international borders. We strive to leverage our collegiate university networks to expand access to education to those experiencing displacement.

Why Choose ROC?

The Answer is Easy.

  • Empowering Refugees

    We are dedicated to provide support, resources, and opportunities to rebuild lives through local and global efforts. By supporting ROC, you contribute directly towards initiatives that empower refugees to pursue education and a greater future.

  • Accountability & Transparency

    Dedicated to be responsible and transparent in all operations of our programs and organization. We provide an explicit list of where the funds are allocated upon donation. We promise to provide regular updates to donors to showcase fund utilization from contributions.


  • Long-Term Sustainability

    Our focus is not just immediate relief efforts, but long-term sustainability and self-dependency for refugees & asylum seekers. We invest in education, training programs, and skill development that enable participants to rebuild their lives with integrity and independence.

  • Collaboration & Partnerships

    Energetically collaborates with top tier Universities, local organizations, community businesses, and stakeholders to maximize our impact. By supporting ROC, you reinforce ROCS value of creating a collaborative environment that maximizes relationships with everyone we encounter.

  • Actively Advocating

    Constantly spreading awareness by regularly organizing advocacy efforts across US campuses in Michigan and Ohio. We host monthly campus chapter meetings, public volunteer events, and public fundraising efforts. Your donation and efforts would help create a more inclusive world for refugees.

  • Dedication Towards Change

    Devoted to finding optimal long-term solutions for those facing resettlement with a driven, yet passionate, outlook while changing negative connotations of forced migration.
